countries = list(covid_dataset_aggregated.index)
maxx_infected_rates = []
for c in countries:
    maxx_infected_rates.append(covid_dataset_aggregated.loc[c].diff().max())
covid_dataset_aggregated["maxx_infected_rate"] = maxx_infected_rates

Conclusion

With The help of Covid-19 Dataset By John Hopkins University and World Health Report, We found the below insigths

  • Infection Rate was more in countries having higher GDP
  • Maximum Infected Rate was more in Populated Countries
  • Countries Having great Social support have more number of Covid-19 Cases
